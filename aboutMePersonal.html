Things I Love

University of Kentucky

One of my favorite things is the University of Kentucky. I've been a Kentucky basketball and football fan for a long time. I go to school at UK and I love it even more now. UK is helping me achieve my goals and I can not thank them enough for that.

Sports

I love sports. I've had a long history with sports as I experimented with all different kinds of sports in my childhood. During high school I played 4 years of tennis and 1 year of golf. In my free time (pre-COVID) you could find me playing any type of sport. I love playing pickup basketball or touch football. I also enjoy watching sports in person or on TV. I love to play fantasy football. Any and all sports entertain me. Some of my favorite sports teams are the Kentucky Wildcats (obviously), Cincinnati Bengals, and Cincinnati Reds.

Working Out

I started working out my junior year of high school and have been in love with it ever since. I am a very competitive person so the feeling of always competing against yourself pushes me. I do not have any pictures to show my love for working out, but it does play a major role in my life.

The Lake

When I was a little kid my family would go to Lake Cumberland in Kentucky and stay at my grandma and grandpa's place. As I got older my family went to the lake every summer with our family friends. I have some of my best memories at the lake. Although it is an unpopular opinion I think I would take the lake over the beach anyday.

Video Games

I enjoy video games because the games themselves are fun, but really what makes me love video games is the time I get to spend with my friends on them. I have spent nights laughing while playing games with my friends. Videos games are one thing that really helped me get through the COVID-19 pandemic.

Computer Science

When I was younger my favorite subject in school was math. Math always came easy to me and I liked how there was always a definitive answer. When I learned what coding and computer science were I really started to enjoy working on school work. Computer science is a passion of mine and I am excited to see where it might take me in my career and life.
